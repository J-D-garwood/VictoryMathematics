About Us

Victory Mathematics began with a simple goal: to make maths make sense. Jimmy, our founder, knows how frustrating school maths can be — he’s been there. But a few great teachers changed the way he saw things, and that experience stuck. He went on to study Honours Engineering at university, and now leads a team helping students feel confident in class, proud of their results, and supported by tutors who care.

Services

We offer 1-on-1 online tutoring across a wide range of high school maths topics, tailored to each student's needs and learning style. Whether you're building foundations or tackling advanced material, we’ve got you covered.

We also support students in both the HSC (Standard, Advanced, Extension 1 & 2) and IB Mathematics (AA & AI, SL and HL) courses.

  • Precalculus
  • Inequalities

  • Polynomials
  • Sequences
